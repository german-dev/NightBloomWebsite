About Me

Teacher, scientist, and chemical engineer

Mother of 2 living girls (and one dead one)

Wife to my beautiful husband

Story-teller activist for abortion rights

Support-person at Ending a Wanted Pregnancy (for TFMR), and Through The Fire (for all womb-grief)

Holding others through grief transformation through somatic love, sex, and relationship coaching.

In 2012, when I lost my daughter Laurel, choosing to terminate a wanted pregnancy for medical reasons, I began a grief-journey that has shaped me in ways I couldn’t even imagine at that time. Service to grieving people and communities has been central to my own healing. I have found an internal landscape of strength, peace, and love beyond anything I could have imagined if grief never cracked me wide open. I hope to help others find the same.

I deeply believe we are already whole and ok, no matter how fragmented we feel. The work is finding all the parts of ourselves we’ve lost and rejected along the way, calling them home to one integrated self. It is a deep honor to hold you on your journey.

If you’d like to find your own space of strength, peace, and love on your own journey, I’m here to hold you through the process and offer tools along the way.

MY COACHING METHOD

Desire

Identify what you want so we know which steps to take to get you there. Sharing positive vision is especially powerful for relationship transformation.

Embody

Feel for the truth beneath the surface and let the body reveal conflicts and blockages.

Integrate

Align all parts of you to your heart’s desire and step into the peace and power of your fully integrated self.
